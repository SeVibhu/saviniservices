✅ Offline & Read-Only App

This Privacy Policy describes how Savini Services ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the TradeWords Translator mobile application (package: com.translang) on Android and iOS devices.

Short version: TradeWords Translator is a fully offline, read-only reference app. It does not connect to the internet during use, does not collect any data, and does not transmit anything from your device. Your privacy is completely protected by design.

1 Information We Collect

TradeWords Translator collects no personal data whatsoever. The app is read-only and fully offline. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect or access your location.
  • We do not record or transmit your searches or translation history.
  • We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not require an account or login to use the app.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or any other device feature.

The app stores your language and country preference locally on your device only. This data is never sent anywhere.

2 Offline & Read-Only Design

TradeWords Translator is built to work entirely without an internet connection. All vocabulary data is bundled within the app itself at installation. You can use the app on a plane, in a remote field, or anywhere without connectivity.

The app is read-only — users browse and search the translation database but cannot create, edit, or submit any content.

No language input or search data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

4 Third-Party Services

TradeWords Translator does not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, or social login services.

5 Children's Privacy

TradeWords Translator is rated Everyone and is suitable for all ages, including school and university students. Because the app collects absolutely no data from any user, it is fully safe for children and complies with child privacy regulations worldwide.

6 GDPR — Users in the EU / EEA

As a Swedish-based service, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because we do not collect or process any personal data, no data subject rights are triggered. There is no data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions, please contact us below.
